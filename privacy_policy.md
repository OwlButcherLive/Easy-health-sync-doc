<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>The <strong>Easy Health Sync</strong> app, developed and operated by <strong>Martin Larroque</strong>, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your health and personal information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the application.</p>

<h2>Information We Collect</h2>

<h3>Health Data</h3>
<p>Easy Health Sync accesses and processes the following health data through Huawei Health Kit and Google Health Connect:</p>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Step Count:</strong> Daily step counts and walking/running activity</li>
    <li><strong>Heart Rate:</strong> Heart rate measurements</li>
    <li><strong>Weight:</strong> Body weight measurements</li>
    <li><strong>Distance:</strong> Distance traveled during activities</li>
    <li><strong>Calories:</strong> Calories burned during activities</li>
    <li><strong>Exercise Sessions:</strong> Workout and activity session data</li>
    <li><strong>Sleep Data:</strong> Sleep duration and quality information</li>
    <li><strong>Blood Pressure:</strong> Blood pressure readings</li>
    <li><strong>Blood Glucose:</strong> Blood glucose level measurements</li>
    <li><strong>Oxygen Saturation (SpO2):</strong> Blood oxygen level readings</li>
    <li><strong>Body Temperature:</strong> Body temperature measurements</li>
    <li><strong>Body Fat Percentage:</strong> Body composition data</li>
    <li><strong>Nutrition:</strong> Dietary and nutrition information</li>
</ul>

<h3>Technical Data</h3>
<p>Easy Health Sync may collect:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Device information (model, operating system version)</li>
    <li>App usage statistics (for improving app performance)</li>
    <li>Error logs and crash reports (for debugging purposes)</li>
</ul>

<h2>How We Use Your Information</h2>
<p>Your health data is used exclusively for:</p>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Data Synchronization:</strong> Syncing health data between Huawei Health and Google Health Connect</li>
    <li><strong>Display:</strong> Showing your health metrics within the app</li>
    <li><strong>Local Processing:</strong> All data processing occurs locally on your device</li>
</ul>

<h2>Data Storage and Security</h2>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Local Storage Only:</strong> All health data is stored locally on your device</li>
    <li><strong>No Cloud Storage:</strong> Your health data is not uploaded to any external servers or cloud services</li>
    <li><strong>No Third-Party Sharing:</strong> Your health data is not shared with third parties, advertisers, or analytics services</li>
    <li><strong>Encryption:</strong> Data transmitted between health platforms uses industry-standard encryption</li>
</ul>

<h2>Your Rights and Control</h2>
<p>You have control over your health data:</p>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Access:</strong> View your health data within the app</li>
    <li><strong>Delete:</strong> Remove data through your device's health platforms (Huawei Health or Google Health Connect)</li>
    <li><strong>Revoke Permissions:</strong> Withdraw app permissions at any time through your device settings</li>
    <li><strong>Data Portability:</strong> Export your data through the respective health platforms</li>
</ul>

<h2>Data Retention</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Health data is retained only as long as you keep it in your device's health platforms</li>
    <li>Uninstalling the app does not delete data from Huawei Health or Google Health Connect</li>
    <li>You must delete data directly from those platforms if desired</li>
</ul>

<h2>Children's Privacy</h2>
<p>Easy Health Sync is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age, and health data from children is not knowingly collected.</p>
